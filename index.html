Our Programs

Programs That Build Futures

Give N Go Education

Provides students with the knowledge and tools needed to thrive in modern careers. Participants gain hands-on experience using industry equipment, technology, and software. Students graduate ready to handle the challenges of work and real life.

Youth Workforce Leadership Development

Allows young people to experience what it means to lead and make a difference in their communities. Participants build confidence, resilience, and leadership skills through hands-on learning and mentorship. Our approach is transparent, supportive, and focused on personal growth.

E.N.D. Empower N Deploy

Give N Go's youth talent pipeline program. Through partnerships with local businesses and sponsors, students gain access to professional resources, mentorship, and career development opportunities. After completing the program, graduates are connected to internships and job opportunities.
